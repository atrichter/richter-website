Summer on the Square

Free Concert Series

Every Friday evening in June, July, and August

Performances are 7:00 to 8:30 p.m. South side of the square, downtown Kirksville Rain Venue: Wrong Daddyâ€™s, 301 W. McPherson.

Summer on the Square is a primary program of the Kirksville Arts Association with the goal to bring the best local and regional musicians to Kirksville and provide a FREE family-friendly entertainment opportunity to the community on the front steps of the historic Kirksville courthouse. Join us each Friday on the square this summer for the best of local and regional entertainment.
